About VVPIET

VVPIET is a premier engineering institution committed to excellence in technical education. Established with a vision to create skilled professionals, the institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, VVPIET prepares students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

Placements at VVPIET

The Training and Placement Cell at VVPIET works tirelessly to provide excellent career opportunities to students. With strong industry partnerships and a dedicated team, we facilitate campus recruitment drives, internships, and skill development programs. Our students have been placed in leading companies across various sectors including IT, manufacturing, consulting, and core engineering.
